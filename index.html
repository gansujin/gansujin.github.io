About

I am a PhD candidate in Biomedical Informatics at Ajou University School of Medicine, advised by Prof. Rae Woong Park. My research focuses on building and validating AI systems that extract clinically meaningful signals from unstructured health records — work that spans clinical NLP, pharmacoepidemiology, and nursing informatics, grounded in OMOP-CDM and real-world evidence methods.

My dissertation develops a modular LLM agent pipeline for early detection of clinical deterioration from Korean inpatient nursing narratives, grounded in Tanner's Clinical Judgment Model.
