Sujin Gan / 간수진

PhD Candidate, RN · Advisor: Prof. Rae Woong Park, MD, PhD

I build and validate AI systems for clinical decision support — focusing on extracting clinically meaningful signals from unstructured health records. My work spans clinical NLP, pharmacoepidemiology, and nursing informatics, grounded in OMOP-CDM and real-world evidence methods. My dissertation develops a modular LLM agent pipeline for early detection of clinical deterioration from Korean inpatient nursing narratives.
